Tentang Yayasan

Yayasan Hidayatul Ummah Kediri adalah lembaga resmi terdaftar di Indonesia di bawah kepemimpinan KH. Moh Siri. Kami menyediakan layanan pendidikan Islam dan kesejahteraan sosial untuk masyarakat dengan integritas profesional dan transparansi.

Visi kami adalah menciptakan generasi Muslim yang beriman, berilmu, dan berakhlak mulia, serta berkontribusi positif bagi masyarakat sekitar.

Yayasan kami aktif dalam berbagai kegiatan keagamaan, pendidikan anak, dan program sosial untuk membantu masyarakat kurang mampu.

About the Foundation

Yayasan Hidayatul Ummah Kediri is an officially registered foundation in Indonesia under the leadership of KH. Moh Siri. We provide Islamic education and social welfare services to the community with professional integrity and transparency.

Our vision is to create a generation of Muslims who are faithful, knowledgeable, and noble in character, and contribute positively to the surrounding community.

Our foundation is active in various religious activities, children's education, and social programs to help underprivileged communities.
